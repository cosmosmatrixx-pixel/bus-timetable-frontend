Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 25, 2024 At HR ROUTE, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our website, we may collect certain information about you, including: Your name, email address, and other contact information Information about your computer or mobile device, including your IP address, browser type, and operating system Usage data, such as pages visited and the time spent on each page Information about your interactions with our website, such as the date and time of your visit, and the referring website

2. How We Use Your Information

We may use the information we collect from you to: Provide you the timetable of buses Respond to your inquiries and requests Analyze and improve our website and services Send you newsletters and other marketing communications Protect our rights and the rights of our users

3. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information with third-party service providers who help us to operate our website and provide our services. These service providers may include hosting providers, payment processors, and email service providers. We may also share your information in response to legal requests, such as subpoenas or court orders.

4. Cookies

We use cookies and other similar technologies to collect information about your usage of our website. Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. We use cookies to personalize your experience, analyze usage patterns, and provide relevant content and advertising.

5. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party sites. 6. Your Choices You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may limit your ability to use certain features of our website.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction. However, no data transmission over the internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. 8. Children's Privacy Our website is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. If we make changes to this policy, we will notify you by posting an updated version on our website. 10. Contact Us If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us here.
